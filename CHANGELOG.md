## vThere is offered the cat2cat procedure to map a categorical variable according to a transition table between two different time points. The transition table should to have a candidate for each category from the targeted for an update period. The main rule is to replicate the observation if it could be assigned to a few categories, then using simple frequencies or statistical methods to approximate probabilities of being assigned to each of them.
